Practive is the **only CA PMS with a functional WhatsApp AI chatbot**, making it the most innovative competitor in client communication. Its custom-domain client portal and Razorpay integration for online payments add to a modern, client-facing focus.

## Key Features

- **WhatsApp AI Chatbot:** Automated client responses, status queries, reminders
- **Client Portal:** Custom domain, branded experience, OTP-less access
- **Razorpay Integration:** Online payment collection directly from invoices
- **Task Management:** Basic task tracking and assignment
- **Billing:** GST-compliant invoicing with online payment links
- **Custom Domain:** White-label portal under the CA firm's domain
- **Mobile App:** Basic mobile access

:::note[WhatsApp AI Is the Moat]
Practive's WhatsApp chatbot is genuinely useful -- clients can ask "What is the status of my ITR?" and get an instant automated response. No other PMS offers this. However, the chatbot is limited to status queries and basic interactions.
:::

## Strengths

1. **WhatsApp AI chatbot** -- genuine differentiator, only PMS with this
2. **Custom domain portal** -- professional client-facing experience
3. **Razorpay integration** -- online payment collection is rare in CA PMS
4. **Modern approach** -- client-first design philosophy
5. **Competitive pricing** -- Rs 75-99/user is below market average

## Weaknesses

| Area | Issue |
|---|---|
| **Shallow PMS** | Task management is basic compared to QwikCA, SimplifyPractice |
| **No compliance depth** | No GST filing, no IT portal, no compliance calendar |
| **No document intelligence** | Zero OCR or extraction capability |
| **No Tally** | No Tally integration |
| **No analytics** | Limited reporting and dashboards |
| **No team management** | No attendance, leave, or HR features |
| **WhatsApp limitations** | Chatbot handles queries but cannot collect documents or trigger workflows |

## CAOS Competitive Strategy

:::tip[How CAOS Wins Against Practive]
- **Deeper WhatsApp integration** -- doc collection, workflow triggers, bulk messaging, not just status queries
- **Full compliance depth** -- GST/ITR/TDS/ROC calendar and auto-fetch
- **Document intelligence** -- the wedge feature Practive completely lacks
- **Tally integration** -- essential for CA workflows
- **Advanced analytics** -- practice insights beyond basic reports
- **Team management** -- attendance, utilization, workload balancing
:::

### Migration Path
Practive users value modern UX and WhatsApp. CAOS must **match or exceed the WhatsApp experience** on day one -- this is non-negotiable for winning these users. The portal and payment features must also be at parity.
