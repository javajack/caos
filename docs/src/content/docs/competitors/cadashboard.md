CA Dashboard is a **Pune-based veteran** of the CA PMS market, offering three distinct product modules targeting CAs, Company Secretaries, and SME businesses. Its tiered pricing model targets mid-size firms willing to pay a premium for comprehensive features.

## Key Features

- **CA Module:** Task management, compliance tracking, client management for CAs
- **CS Module:** Dedicated module for Company Secretaries with MCA workflows
- **SME Module:** Business management tools for SME clients of CA firms
- **Task Management:** Multi-level task tracking with assignment and deadlines
- **Billing:** GST-compliant invoicing with tiered pricing support
- **Client Management:** Client master with multi-entity support
- **Reports:** Practice reports and compliance dashboards

:::note[Three-Module Strategy]
The CA+CS+SME approach is unique but creates a fragmented product. Each module feels like a separate tool rather than a unified platform. Firms that need all three pay significantly more.
:::

## Strengths

1. **CS module** -- unique coverage for Company Secretaries, rare in the market
2. **Longevity** -- operating since ~2014, proven stability
3. **Mid-market positioning** -- not competing in the crowded Rs 100/user space
4. **Multi-entity** -- handles firms with CA + CS practices

## CAOS Competitive Strategy

:::tip[How CAOS Wins Against CA Dashboard]
- **Unified platform** vs. fragmented three-module approach
- **Modern UX** vs. decade-old interface
- **Better value** -- more features at competitive per-user pricing
- **AI and WhatsApp** -- capabilities CA Dashboard cannot offer
- **Mobile-first** -- app access they do not provide
- **CS workflows** can be a CAOS module rather than a separate product
:::

### Migration Path
CA Dashboard users likely have significant historical data. Offer **CSV/Excel import tools** and highlight the unified platform advantage -- "one tool instead of three modules."
