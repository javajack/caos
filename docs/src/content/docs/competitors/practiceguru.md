## Overview

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Founded** | ~2020 |

| **Pricing** | Rs 499/year |

| **Users** | Growing |
| **Funding** | Bootstrapped |
| **Key Differentiator** | Practice growth and marketing tools, not traditional PMS |

PracticeGuru occupies a **unique niche** in the CA ecosystem -- it is not a practice management system but a **practice growth platform** focused on branding, client acquisition, and professional development for CAs.

## Key Features

- **Personal Branding Tools:** Website builder, social media templates, CA profile pages
- **CRM & Lead Management:** Track prospects, manage referrals, client pipeline
- **Training & Learning:** Video courses, webinars, CA skill development content
- **Client Communication:** Newsletters, greeting cards, branded communications
- **Networking:** CA community features and peer connections
- **Resource Library:** Templates, checklists, and practice resources

:::note[Not a PMS]
PracticeGuru deliberately avoids competing with traditional PMS tools. It focuses exclusively on the "front office" -- getting clients, building reputation, and growing revenue -- while leaving task and compliance management to other tools.
:::

## Strengths

1. **Ultra-low pricing** -- Rs 499/year is essentially a no-brainer purchase
2. **Unique positioning** -- only product focused on CA practice growth
3. **No direct PMS competition** -- complementary to existing tools
4. **Marketing tools** -- fills a gap no other CA tool addresses
5. **Training content** -- ongoing value through learning resources

## Weaknesses

| Area | Issue |
|---|---|
| **Not a PMS** | Cannot manage tasks, compliance, billing, or team |
| **Limited scope** | Growth tools are shallow compared to dedicated marketing platforms |
| **No compliance** | Zero compliance or tax features |
| **No integrations** | Does not connect with any PMS or accounting tool |
| **Small impact** | Nice-to-have, not must-have for running a practice |

## CAOS Competitive Strategy

:::tip[CAOS Approach to PracticeGuru's Niche]
PracticeGuru is not a direct competitor -- it is a **feature gap indicator**. The growth tools it offers (branding, CRM, client acquisition) are missing from every PMS. CAOS can absorb these capabilities as a module:
- Built-in website/portal builder for CAs
- CRM with lead tracking and conversion pipeline
- Client referral program management
- Branded communication templates
:::

### Coexistence vs. Absorption
PracticeGuru can coexist with CAOS in the short term. Long-term, CAOS should incorporate growth features to eliminate the need for a separate tool. At Rs 499/year, PracticeGuru is too cheap to actively compete against -- instead, **learn from what it reveals about CA needs**.
