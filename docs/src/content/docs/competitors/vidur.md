## Overview

|---|---|

| **Pricing** | Freemium |

| **Users** | 20,000+ CAs |
| **Funding** | Unknown |
| **Key Differentiator** | AI tax research, 250+ commentaries, ICAI recognized, WhatsApp bot |

Vidur is an **AI-powered tax research platform** that gives CAs instant access to tax knowledge from **250+ commentaries, case laws, circulars, and notifications**. Recognized by ICAI with 20,000+ CA users, it has become the go-to research assistant for Indian tax professionals.

## Key Features

- **AI Tax Research:** Natural language queries answered from 250+ tax sources
- **Commentary Database:** Access to leading tax commentaries and textbooks
- **Case Law Search:** AI-powered search across tax tribunal and court decisions
- **Circular/Notification Index:** Complete index of CBDT/CBIC circulars
- **WhatsApp Bot:** Ask tax questions via WhatsApp and get AI responses
- **ICAI Recognition:** Endorsed by the Institute of Chartered Accountants
- **Mobile App:** Research on the go via mobile application
- **Citation Generation:** Proper legal citation for research findings

:::note[Research Tool, Not PMS]
Vidur is a knowledge and research platform, not a practice management tool. It answers "what does the law say?" but does not help manage client tasks, deadlines, or billing. However, its AI research capability is a feature that PMS tools should integrate.
:::

## Strengths

1. **20K+ CAs** -- significant adoption in the CA community
2. **ICAI recognition** -- institutional credibility
3. **250+ sources** -- comprehensive tax knowledge base
4. **WhatsApp bot** -- accessible via the platform CAs already use daily
5. **AI-powered** -- genuine AI capability for research queries
6. **Freemium** -- free tier drives adoption

## Weaknesses

| Area | Issue |
|---|---|
| **Not a PMS** | No task, client, or practice management |
| **Research-only** | Cannot apply research findings to client work |
| **No document intelligence** | Does not process client documents |
| **No compliance automation** | No filing or compliance workflows |
| **No billing** | No invoicing or fee management |
| **No Tally** | No accounting integration |

## CAOS Competitive Strategy

:::tip[How CAOS Approaches Vidur]
Vidur is a **feature inspiration**, not a competitor. CAOS should:
- Build AI research as a module within the PMS (ask questions in context of a client's data)
- Integrate Vidur's WhatsApp bot approach into CAOS's WhatsApp channel
- Go beyond research to **advisory intelligence** -- proactive suggestions, not just Q&A
- Partner with or license Vidur's research database rather than building from scratch
:::

### Potential Partnership
Vidur's research database + CAOS's practice management = powerful combination. A partnership where Vidur powers the research layer within CAOS would give both products an advantage over standalone competitors.

### Key Talking Points
- "Vidur answers your tax questions. CAOS answers them AND creates the task, tracks the deadline, and bills the client."
- "Research should live inside your workflow, not in a separate browser tab"
