Bizalys stands out with the **largest pre-built compliance workflow library** in the market -- over 600 templates covering not just standard GST/ITR/TDS but also niche areas like **FEMA compliance, tribunal hearings, and trust regulations**. Built by practicing CAs, it reflects deep domain knowledge.

## Key Features

- **600+ Compliance Workflows:** Pre-configured templates for every Indian compliance
- **FEMA Compliance:** Rare coverage of foreign exchange regulations
- **Tribunal/Hearing Management:** Track and manage tax tribunal proceedings
- **Trust & NGO Compliance:** Workflows for Section 12A, 80G entities
- **Task Management:** Compliance-linked task assignment and tracking
- **Client Management:** Client master with multi-compliance mapping
- **Billing:** Basic invoicing and outstanding tracking
- **Calendar:** Comprehensive compliance deadline calendar

:::note[Compliance Depth Champion]
Bizalys has the deepest compliance coverage in the market. While others focus on GST and ITR, Bizalys covers FEMA, hearings, trusts, cooperatives, and other niche compliance areas. This makes it invaluable for firms with diverse compliance practices.
:::
