Digital Doc Sys, also known as **Intelligere**, is an **Ahmedabad-based Tally automation engine** that serves 6,000+ MSMEs. It automates the flow of business documents (invoices, purchase orders, bank statements) into Tally, reducing manual data entry for accountants and business owners.

## Key Features

- **Tally Automation:** Automated voucher creation in Tally from documents
- **Invoice Processing:** Extract and process purchase and sales invoices
- **Document Classification:** Automatic categorization of business documents
- **Bulk Upload:** Process large batches of documents at once
- **MSME Focus:** Workflows designed for small and medium enterprises
- **Data Validation:** Cross-checking extracted data for accuracy
- **Multi-Format:** Handles PDF, images, and scanned documents

:::note[Tally-Centric Approach]
Digital Doc Sys / Intelligere is laser-focused on the Tally ecosystem. Everything flows into Tally -- it does not support other accounting platforms. This deep Tally focus gives it reliability within that niche but limits its market.
:::

## Strengths

1. **6K+ MSMEs** -- proven market traction in Gujarat's MSME ecosystem
2. **Deep Tally automation** -- reliable document-to-Tally pipeline
3. **Ahmedabad base** -- strong in Gujarat's business community
4. **Document classification** -- automated categorization is useful

## Weaknesses

| **No PMS** | No practice management features |
| **No compliance** | No GST/ITR/TDS automation |

| **No AI research** | No tax research or advisory features |

## CAOS Competitive Strategy

:::tip[CAOS Approach to Digital Doc Sys]
Digital Doc Sys validates document-to-accounting automation demand. CAOS should:
- Build Tally automation as a core integration module
- Extend beyond Tally to support multiple accounting platforms
- Target CAs who manage the MSMEs that use Digital Doc Sys
- Include document classification and validation in the extraction pipeline
:::

Digital Doc Sys users' accountants are potential CAOS customers. The tool solves a problem at the business level; CAOS solves the equivalent problem at the practice level.
