Zoho Practice is the practice management module from **Zoho Corporation**, one of India's largest SaaS companies. Backed by massive resources and the broader Zoho ecosystem (CRM, Books, Sign, etc.), it enters the CA market with brand credibility but **zero India-specific compliance features**.

## Key Features

- **Task Management:** Workflow automation, recurring tasks, templates
- **Client Management:** Full CRM integration via Zoho ecosystem
- **Billing:** Integrated with Zoho Books and Zoho Invoice
- **Client Portal:** Full-featured portal via Zoho ecosystem
- **Mobile App:** Available through Zoho mobile apps
- **Zoho Ecosystem:** Seamless integration with 50+ Zoho products
- **Document Management:** Via Zoho WorkDrive
- **Time Tracking:** Built-in time tracking and billable hours

:::caution[No India Compliance]
Despite being an Indian company, Zoho Practice has **zero India-specific compliance features** -- no GST workflows, no ITR tracking, no TDS management, no ROC compliance, no IT portal integration. It is a generic practice management tool with no localization.
:::

## Strengths

2. **ICAI 3-year free offer** -- aggressive acquisition strategy
3. **Enterprise backing** -- $1B+ company with massive engineering resources
4. **Brand trust** -- Zoho is a recognized, trusted Indian brand
5. **Modern UX** -- clean, professional interface
6. **Scalability** -- proven infrastructure for large deployments

## CAOS Competitive Strategy

:::tip[How CAOS Wins Against Zoho Practice]
- **India compliance built-in** -- GST, ITR, TDS, ROC workflows are CAOS's core
- **Tally integration** -- essential for Indian CAs, Zoho does not offer it
- **WhatsApp** -- primary communication channel for Indian clients
- **Document intelligence** -- 26AS, AIS, Form 16 extraction built-in
- **Purpose-built** -- designed for Indian CAs, not adapted from global template
- **Better value** -- more relevant features at lower price point
:::

### Threat Assessment
Zoho's 3-year free ICAI offer is a **land grab strategy**. The risk is that CAs adopt Zoho Practice for free, build habits around it, and then Zoho adds India compliance features using their massive engineering team. CAOS must establish product-market fit **before Zoho localizes**.

### Key Talking Points
- "Zoho is a great company building a global product. CAOS is purpose-built for Indian CAs."
- "Free for 3 years means you get 3 years of no GST workflows, no ITR tracking, no Tally"
- "When the free period ends, you will need to migrate anyway -- start with the right tool"
