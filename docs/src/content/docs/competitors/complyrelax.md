| **Key Differentiator** | MCA/ROC filing specialist, ICSI partnership, AI compliance check |

ComplyRelax is a **niche compliance platform** focused on **MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) filings** and company secretarial work. Its partnership with **ICSI (Institute of Company Secretaries of India)** gives it credibility in the CS segment, similar to SimplifyPractice's ICAI relationship.

## Key Features

- **MCA Filing:** Automated MCA form preparation and filing
- **ROC Compliance:** Complete ROC compliance tracking and management
- **AI Compliance Check:** AI-powered compliance validation before filing
- **Annual Filing Calendar:** Pre-configured deadlines for MCA/ROC filings
- **Document Generation:** Automated board resolution and minutes templates
- **Company Master:** Entity management with director and shareholder tracking
- **ICSI Partnership:** Recognized by the Institute of Company Secretaries

:::note[CS Focus, Not CA]
ComplyRelax targets Company Secretaries and MCA compliance, not CA practice management. It does not handle GST, ITR, TDS, or typical CA workflows. However, many CA firms also handle company secretarial work.
:::

## Strengths

1. **MCA specialization** -- deepest MCA filing capability in the market
2. **ICSI partnership** -- institutional credibility with Company Secretaries
3. **AI compliance check** -- validates filings before submission to catch errors
4. **Template library** -- board resolutions, minutes, and statutory documents

## Weaknesses

| Area | Issue |
|---|---|
| **Narrow scope** | MCA/ROC only, no GST/ITR/TDS |
| **Not CA PMS** | No task management, billing, or practice features |
| **No Tally** | No accounting integration |
| **No WhatsApp** | No client communication |
| **No client portal** | No client-facing interface |
| **Limited market** | CS segment is smaller than CA segment |

## CAOS Competitive Strategy

:::tip[CAOS Approach to ComplyRelax]
ComplyRelax validates the need for **MCA/ROC compliance automation** within a CA platform. CAOS should:
- Build MCA/ROC workflows as a module (many CA firms handle company secretarial work)
- AI compliance checking is a feature worth replicating for all filing types
- Consider the CS market as an expansion opportunity
:::

### Feature Inspiration
ComplyRelax's AI compliance check -- validating filings before submission -- is a pattern CAOS should adopt across all compliance types (GST, ITR, TDS, ROC). Pre-submission validation catches errors that cost CAs time and client trust.
