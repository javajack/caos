Welcome to the CAOS market research documentation. This is a comprehensive analysis of the Indian Chartered Accountant practice management software market -- covering 26 competing products, market sizing, competitive positioning, product strategy, business model, and go-to-market approach.

## What Is CAOS?

CAOS is a vision for a unified **CA Practice Management + CRM + Document Intelligence** platform for Indian CA firms. It works on a **layer above Tally** -- managing workflow, clients, communication, documents, billing, and intelligence -- without replacing any existing accounting software. If Tally is the engine, CAOS is the cockpit.

:::tip[Key Insight]
With 1,00,138 registered CA firms in India and less than 2% using dedicated practice management software, this is a significant greenfield opportunity. The market is deeply underpenetrated despite 20+ competitors, because no single product has cracked the code of combining document intelligence, WhatsApp-native workflow, and practice management in one platform.
:::
