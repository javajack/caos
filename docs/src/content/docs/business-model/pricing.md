## Pricing Philosophy

CAOS pricing follows three principles:

1. **Free tier earns trust.** CAs must experience value before paying. The free tier is a real product, not a demo.
2. **Pricing scales with firm size.** Solo practitioners and 50-person firms have fundamentally different willingness to pay. Tiers reflect this.
3. **Price below perceived value.** If CAOS saves a firm INR 50,000/year in staff time, pricing at INR 6,000-24,000/year is an easy decision.

:::note
Indian B2B SaaS pricing must account for extreme price sensitivity in the SMB segment. CAs evaluate software ROI in terms of staff hours saved and deadlines not missed -- not feature counts. All pricing communication should lead with ROI, not features.
:::

## Annual Billing Discounts

| Tier | Monthly Billing | Annual Billing | Effective Monthly | Savings |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Professional | INR 499 | INR 3,588 | INR 299 | 40% (INR 2,400/yr) |
| Business | INR 1,999 | INR 14,388 | INR 1,199 | 40% (INR 9,600/yr) |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Custom | 25-30% (negotiated) |

:::note
The 40% annual discount is aggressive by SaaS standards (typical is 15-25%). This is deliberate. CAs are highly price-sensitive and make annual budgeting decisions. A steep annual discount drives annual commitments, which reduce churn and improve cash flow predictability. The effective price after discount is the "real" price; the monthly price is the anchor.
:::

## Firm-Level Pricing by Client Volume

For firms that do not fit cleanly into standard tiers, CAOS offers client-volume-based pricing.

| Client Volume | Recommended Tier | Price Point | Per-Client Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1-15 | Free | INR 0 | INR 0 |
| 16-50 | Professional | INR 299-499/mo | INR 6-33/client/mo |
| 51-100 | Business | INR 1,199-1,999/mo | INR 12-39/client/mo |
| 101-200 | Business | INR 1,199-1,999/mo | INR 6-20/client/mo |
| 201-500 | Enterprise | INR 5,000-10,000/mo | INR 10-50/client/mo |
| 500+ | Enterprise | Custom | Custom |

### Per-Client Economics

The per-client cost decreases as firm size increases, but the absolute price increases. This aligns with:
- **Solo CAs:** Need low absolute cost (even if per-client cost is higher)
- **Large firms:** Care about per-client efficiency (absolute cost is less sensitive)

:::tip
In sales conversations with mid and large firms, frame pricing as per-client cost. "Less than INR 20 per client per month for complete practice management" is more compelling than "INR 1,999 per month for your firm."
:::

## Pricing Communication Strategy

### Lead with ROI, Not Price

| Segment | ROI Message |
|---|---|
| Solo CA | "Save 10+ hours per week on document processing alone" |
| Small firm | "Your team spends INR 1 lakh/year on manual document work. CAOS costs INR 3,588/year." |
| Mid firm | "Reduce missed deadlines by 90%. One avoided penalty pays for CAOS for a year." |
| Large firm | "Complete practice visibility across branches for less than the cost of one intern." |

### Pricing Page Structure

1. **Hero:** "Plans that grow with your practice"
2. **Tier cards:** Free, Professional, Business, Enterprise side by side
3. **Feature comparison table:** Full feature comparison below cards
4. **ROI calculator:** Interactive tool: "Enter your client count and team size. See your savings."
5. **FAQ:** Address common objections (data security, Tally compatibility, migration)
6. **Social proof:** Testimonials from beta users (added post-Phase 1)

## Summary

CAOS pricing is designed to maximize adoption through a generous free tier, convert through clear ROI at accessible price points, and expand revenue as firms grow. The 40% annual discount drives commitments. The per-client framing makes Business tier compelling for growing firms. Competitive positioning is strong: CAOS offers more value (AI + WhatsApp + practice management) at comparable or lower price points than every alternative.
