# CA Dashboard - Deep Dive

## Overview
- **Website:** https://cadashboard.com
- **Category:** Practice Management (Multi-professional)
- **Founded:** ~2014
- **HQ:** Pune, Maharashtra
- **Company:** CA Dashboard
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** ~5-10
- **Users/Customers:** 440+ firms

## Product Description
CA Dashboard is one of the oldest players in the Indian practice management space, operating since approximately 2014. It features a unique three-module architecture serving CAs, Company Secretaries (CS), and SMEs -- making it one of the few platforms attempting to serve multiple professional segments.

The platform includes e-signature capabilities, compliance tracking, and multi-module workflows. At Rs 10,000-45,000 per year, it is positioned at a premium price point relative to newer competitors charging Rs 100/user/month. This pricing and the 440+ firm user base suggest it serves primarily established, mid-size firms.

Despite its longevity, CA Dashboard appears to be stagnating. The dated UI, limited integrations, and relatively modest user base after 10+ years indicate challenges in product evolution and market expansion. The premium pricing also creates vulnerability against newer, more affordable alternatives.

## Key Features
- CA module (task, client, compliance management)
- CS module (company law compliance)
- SME module (business management tools)
- E-signature capabilities
- Task management and assignment
- Client management
- Compliance deadline tracking
- Document management
- Team management
- Billing and invoicing
- MIS reports and dashboards
- Multi-branch support
- Role-based access control

## Target Segment
- Mid-size CA firms (10-50+ employees)
- CS firms needing practice management
- Multi-discipline practices (CA + CS)
- Established firms willing to pay premium pricing

## Pricing
| Plan | Price | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| Basic | Rs 10,000/year | Single module, limited features |
| Professional | Rs 25,000/year | Multi-module access |
| Enterprise | Rs 45,000/year | Full suite, all modules |

Premium annual pricing, significantly more than per-user competitors.

## Integrations
- E-signature (built-in)
- No Tally integration
- No WhatsApp integration
- No GST portal auto-fetch
- No payment gateway
- Limited third-party integrations

## Strengths
- Long market presence (since ~2014)
- Three-module architecture serving CA + CS + SME
- E-signature capabilities
- 440+ firm user base
- Multi-discipline practice support
- Comprehensive feature set for established firms

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- Dated user interface
- Premium pricing (Rs 10K-45K/year) vs. Rs 100/user/month competitors
- Only 440+ firms after 10+ years -- slow growth
- No Tally integration
- No WhatsApp integration
- No GST portal auto-fetch
- No AI or modern automation
- No client portal
- No payment collection
- Stagnating product evolution
- Three-module approach dilutes focus on CA-specific needs

## Unique Selling Points
- Three-module architecture (CA + CS + SME)
- E-signature built-in
- Multi-discipline practice support

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW
- **Where they compete:** Core practice management for mid-size firms, compliance tracking, e-signature
- **Where CAOS can win:** Modern UX, lower pricing (Rs 100/user/month vs. Rs 10K-45K/year), integrations (Tally, WhatsApp, GST), AI automation, client portal. CA Dashboard's stagnation and premium pricing make its 440+ firm users potential conversion targets for CAOS. Focus on the "modern replacement" narrative.
