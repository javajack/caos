# Bizalys - Deep Dive

## Overview
- **Website:** https://bizalys.com
- **Category:** Compliance Workflow Management
- **Founded:** 2020
- **HQ:** Nashik, Maharashtra
- **Company:** Bizalys (built by CAs)
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** ~5-10
- **Users/Customers:** 200+ firms

## Product Description
Bizalys is a compliance workflow management platform built by CAs, with an impressive 600+ pre-configured compliance workflows covering a wide range of Indian regulatory requirements. This library of ready-made workflows is its primary differentiator -- new users can immediately start tracking compliance without manual setup.

The platform goes beyond standard GST/ITR compliance to include FEMA compliance and hearing management, which are niche areas that most competitors ignore. This suggests the founding CAs had experience with cross-border transactions and regulatory hearings.

At Rs 6,000/year with 200+ firms and estimated Rs 10 lakh revenue, Bizalys is a small but focused operation. Its workflow library is valuable but the limited user base and revenue suggest challenges in scaling or go-to-market execution.

## Unique Selling Points

- Built by CAs with domain expertise

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW
- **Where they compete:** Compliance workflow management, pre-built compliance calendars
- **Where CAOS can win:** CAOS can replicate the compliance workflow library (it's a content/data asset, not technology) while offering everything Bizalys lacks -- integrations, WhatsApp, Tally, GST auto-fetch, client portal, billing. Bizalys's slow growth (200 firms in 4 years) suggests the product alone isn't compelling enough. CAOS should study and learn from the 600+ workflow templates as a content asset to build.
