# PracticeGuru - Deep Dive

## Overview
- **Website:** https://practiceguru.pro
- **Category:** Practice Growth / CRM / Training
- **Founded:** ~2019-2020
- **HQ:** India (city not confirmed)
- **Company:** PracticeGuru
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** 14
- **Users/Customers:** 3,400+ clients

## Product Description
PracticeGuru is a practice GROWTH platform for CAs, focusing on branding, CRM, and training rather than traditional practice management. It helps CA firms build their brand, attract clients through marketing tools, and upskill through training programs -- essentially the business development side of running a CA practice.

The platform includes CRM capabilities, though it is limited to single-user CRM only, which severely limits its utility for firms with multiple partners or sales staff. At Rs 499-2,999 per year, it is very affordably priced, positioning it as a low-commitment tool.

With 3,400+ clients and a 14-person team, PracticeGuru has found a niche audience. However, its focus on growth/marketing rather than operational management means it does not directly compete with practice management tools. It is more of a complementary service.

## Key Features
- Practice branding tools and templates
- CRM for client relationship management (single-user only)
- Training and upskilling programs for CAs
- Marketing collateral creation
- Client acquisition strategies and tools
- Social media content templates
- Email marketing tools
- Website and online presence guidance
- Webinar and event management
- Community and networking

## Target Segment
- Solo practitioners looking to grow their practice
- Small CA firms wanting better branding
- CAs seeking client acquisition tools
- Professionals wanting training and upskilling

## Integrations
- No accounting software integrations
- No Tally or GST portal
- No WhatsApp automation
- Basic email and social media tools

## Strengths
- Unique positioning in practice GROWTH (not operations)
- 3,400+ clients show market demand for growth tools
- Very affordable pricing
- 14-person team is reasonable for the product scope
- Addresses the "how to get more clients" pain point

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- Not a practice management tool -- no task, compliance, or workflow management
- CRM is single-user only -- not suitable for firm-wide use
- No operational features (billing, document management, team management)
- No compliance tracking or deadline management
- No integrations with CA workflow tools
- Growth/marketing tools are generic, not deeply CA-specific

## Unique Selling Points
- Only platform focused on CA practice GROWTH (branding + CRM + training)
- Very low price point creates easy adoption
- Addresses business development, not operations

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW
- **Where they compete:** PracticeGuru does not compete with CAOS on practice management. It competes for mindshare and wallet in the "tools for CAs" category.
- **Where CAOS can win:** If CAOS includes basic CRM and client acquisition features, it could subsume PracticeGuru's value. CAOS's client portal and communication features already address some of the "client relationship" needs PracticeGuru targets.
