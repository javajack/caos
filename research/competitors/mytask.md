## Product Description
MyTask is a comprehensive CA practice management system that has been in the market since 2014, making it one of the older players in the Indian CA PMS space. It covers a wide range of practice management needs including task management, client management, compliance tracking, and team management.

A distinctive feature is its DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) physical tracking capability -- it tracks which physical DSC token is with which employee, solving a real logistical challenge for CA firms that manage dozens or hundreds of client DSCs. MyTask also includes GST and ITR status verification, automatically checking filing status on government portals.

Despite its long history and comprehensive feature set, MyTask has mixed reviews averaging around 3 out of 5 stars. The minimum requirement of 6 users suggests it targets mid-size firms rather than solo practitioners, and its pricing of Rs 95-130/user/month places it in the competitive mid-range.

## Integrations

## Strengths
- Long market presence (since 2014) -- battle-tested
- DSC physical tracking is unique and solves a real problem
- GST/ITR auto-verification from government portals
- Comprehensive feature set covering most PM needs
- Established brand in the CA community

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- Mixed user reviews (3/5 average) suggest quality issues
- Minimum 6-user requirement excludes small firms and solos
- Dated user interface
- No WhatsApp integration
- No Tally integration
- No client-facing portal
- No AI or automation beyond status checking
- No payment collection or billing automation
- Stagnating product -- limited innovation over 10+ years

## Unique Selling Points
- DSC physical tracking (who has which token)
- GST and ITR status auto-verification
- Decade-long market presence

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** MEDIUM
- **Where they compete:** Core practice management -- task management, client management, compliance tracking, team management. Direct competitor in the CA PMS space.
- **Where CAOS can win:** Modern UX (MyTask is dated), no minimum user requirement (serve solos too), WhatsApp integration, Tally integration, client portal, AI automation, billing/payment collection. MyTask's 3/5 reviews indicate dissatisfied users who could be converted. CAOS should specifically target MyTask users as a switching segment.
