# SimplifyPractice - Deep Dive

## Overview
- **Website:** https://simplifypractice.com
- **Category:** Practice Management
- **Founded:** ~2018
- **HQ:** Mumbai, Maharashtra
- **Company:** Vritti Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** ~10-15
- **Users/Customers:** Unknown (significant due to ICAI MOU)

## Product Description
SimplifyPractice is a practice management solution specifically built for Indian Chartered Accountants. It has a notable MOU with ICAI (Institute of Chartered Accountants of India), which gives it significant institutional credibility and distribution advantage among CA professionals.

The platform covers core practice management workflows including task management, client management, employee tracking, and compliance deadline management. Its employee monitoring feature is a distinctive addition that appeals to firm owners wanting visibility into staff productivity.

Despite its institutional backing, the product suffers from a dated user experience and lacks integrations with popular accounting and communication tools, limiting its effectiveness in modern CA workflows.

## Key Features
- Task management and assignment
- Client database management
- Compliance deadline tracking and alerts
- Employee monitoring and productivity tracking
- Time tracking
- Document management
- MIS reports and dashboards
- Multi-branch support
- Role-based access control
- Mobile app availability
- ICAI MOU-backed distribution

## Target Segment
- Small to mid-size CA firms (5-50 employees)
- ICAI member CAs seeking institutional-endorsed tools
- Firms wanting employee monitoring capabilities

## Pricing
| Plan | Price | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| Per User | Rs 100/user/month | All features included |

Pricing is straightforward per-user, making it accessible for small firms but costs scale linearly with team size.

## Integrations
- No significant third-party integrations
- No Tally integration
- No WhatsApp integration
- No GST portal integration
- No payment gateway integration

## Strengths
- ICAI MOU provides strong institutional credibility and distribution
- Employee monitoring feature is a unique differentiator
- Simple per-user pricing model
- Established presence in the market
- Specifically designed for Indian CA workflows

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- Dated user experience and interface design
- No integrations with Tally, WhatsApp, or other tools CAs commonly use
- No GST/ITR auto-fetch capabilities
- No client-facing portal
- No billing or invoicing features reported
- No AI or automation features
- Limited innovation trajectory
- Relies heavily on ICAI endorsement rather than product quality

## Unique Selling Points
- Only PMS with an official ICAI MOU/partnership
- Built-in employee monitoring (screen tracking, activity logs)
- Institutional distribution channel through ICAI events and communications

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** MEDIUM
- **Where they compete:** Core practice management (task, client, compliance tracking) at same Rs 100/user/month price point
- **Where CAOS can win:** Modern UX, integrations (Tally, WhatsApp, GST portal), AI-powered automation, client portal, billing/invoicing -- essentially everywhere except institutional backing. CAOS should focus on being the "product-led" alternative to SimplifyPractice's "institution-led" approach.
