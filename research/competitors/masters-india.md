## Product Description
Masters India is a GST Suvidha Provider (GSP) and enterprise-grade GST compliance platform. It provides API-based GST solutions that large enterprises and ERP systems integrate with for e-invoicing, e-way bills, GST filing, and reconciliation. With over 9 crore Invoice Reference Numbers (IRNs) processed, it is one of the largest GST infrastructure providers in India.

The company integrates with major enterprise systems including SAP, Oracle, and Tally, serving as the compliance backbone for large organizations. With 218 employees, it is substantially larger than any CA practice management startup.

Masters India is NOT a CA practice management tool. It is enterprise GST infrastructure. Its relevance to the CA market is indirect -- as the GST compliance backbone that many of the CAs' enterprise clients use, it sits in a different layer of the compliance stack.

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW
- **Where they compete:** Masters India does not compete with CAOS. It operates in the enterprise GST infrastructure layer, while CAOS is CA practice management.
