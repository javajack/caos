- **Company:** ComplyRelax
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** ~5-10
- **Users/Customers:** Unknown

## Product Description
ComplyRelax is a specialized compliance management tool focused on MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs) annual filing requirements. It positions itself as an AI-powered compliance checker with 98% risk detection capability, helping Company Secretaries and CAs manage the complex web of MCA filings, deadlines, and regulatory requirements.

The platform has secured a partnership with ICSI (Institute of Company Secretaries of India), which gives it institutional credibility in the CS community. This CS-focused positioning is important -- while CAs do handle some MCA work, the primary audience is Company Secretaries.

ComplyRelax addresses a real compliance gap but is narrowly focused on MCA/company law compliance rather than the broader CA practice management space. Its pricing ranges from Rs 3,000 to Rs 62,500 per year, suggesting tiered plans based on company count or feature access.

## Key Features
- MCA annual filing compliance tracking
- AI compliance check with 98% risk detection
- Filing deadline management and alerts
- Document preparation for MCA forms
- Compliance gap analysis
- Board meeting and AGM tracking
- Director KYC management

- Multi-company management
- ICSI partnership/endorsement

## Strengths
- Deep specialization in MCA compliance
- AI-powered risk detection (98% claimed)
- ICSI partnership provides institutional credibility
- Addresses a genuine compliance pain point
- Established since 2020 with track record

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- CS-focused, not CA-focused
- Narrow scope -- only MCA/company law compliance
- No practice management features (tasks, clients, team)
- No accounting or GST/ITR features
- No client portal or communication tools
- No integrations with CA workflow tools
- Premium pricing for the top tier

## Unique Selling Points
- Only tool with deep MCA compliance specialization and AI risk detection
- ICSI partnership
- Covers niche compliance area others ignore

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW
- **Where they compete:** MCA compliance tracking (a subset of compliance CAs may handle)
- **Where CAOS can win:** CAOS targets the full CA practice management workflow, not just MCA compliance. If CAOS includes basic MCA filing reminders, it covers the overlap. ComplyRelax is too niche and CS-focused to threaten a CA-centric PMS.
