# Vidur - Deep Dive

## Overview
- **Website:** https://vidur.in
- **Category:** AI Tax & Legal Research
- **Founded:** 2022
- **HQ:** Gurgaon, Haryana
- **Company:** Vidur
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** ~10-15
- **Users/Customers:** 20,000+ CAs

## Product Description
Vidur is an AI-powered tax and legal research platform that helps CAs quickly find relevant case laws, commentaries, and legal provisions. With access to 250+ commentaries and extensive case law databases, it serves as a digital research assistant for tax professionals.

The platform has a notable partnership with ICAI, giving it institutional distribution and credibility. With 20,000+ CAs using the platform, it has achieved significant adoption, suggesting the product addresses a real need in the tax research workflow.

Vidur also offers a WhatsApp bot for quick research queries, meeting CAs where they already spend time. Priced between Rs 1,999 and Rs 9,999 per year, it is positioned as an affordable research tool. Critically, Vidur is NOT a practice management tool -- it is a research and drafting tool.

## Key Features
- AI-powered tax research (Income Tax, GST, Company Law)
- Access to 250+ commentaries and digests
- Case law search and analysis
- Legal provision lookup
- AI-assisted drafting of opinions and submissions
- WhatsApp bot for quick queries
- Bookmark and organize research
- Citation management
- ICAI partnership
- Multi-device access

## Target Segment
- CAs doing tax advisory and litigation
- Tax consultants and lawyers
- CS professionals needing legal research
- Students preparing for CA exams

## Integrations
- WhatsApp (research bot)

## Strengths
- Strong traction with 20K+ CAs
- ICAI partnership for credibility and distribution
- AI-powered research is genuinely useful
- WhatsApp bot is accessible and convenient
- Comprehensive database (250+ commentaries)
- Affordable pricing

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- Not a practice management tool at all
- No task, client, or compliance management
- No workflow or team collaboration features
- No accounting or bookkeeping capabilities
- Narrow use case (research and drafting only)
- Value depends on database comprehensiveness

## Unique Selling Points
- AI-powered tax research with 250+ commentaries
- WhatsApp bot for instant research access
- ICAI partnership
- Large user base (20K+ CAs) validates product-market fit

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW
- **Where they compete:** Vidur does not compete with CAOS. It is a research tool, not a practice management tool. However, it captures CA attention and wallet share.
- **Where CAOS can win:** Completely different product categories. Vidur could be an integration partner -- imagine CAOS linking to Vidur research from within compliance tasks. The 20K+ user base could also be a distribution channel for CAOS through co-marketing.
