# Finexo - Deep Dive

## Overview
- **Website:** https://finexo.in
- **Category:** Practice Management
- **Founded:** February 2026
- **HQ:** Jaipur, Rajasthan
- **Company:** Finexo
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** ~3-5
- **Users/Customers:** 800+ firms

## Product Description
Finexo is a very new practice management system for CAs, launched in February 2026. Its standout feature is automatic task generation from return types -- when a CA onboards a client and specifies which returns they file, Finexo automatically creates the recurring compliance tasks with correct deadlines.

The platform also includes a website builder with a built-in client portal, allowing CA firms to have a professional web presence with integrated client login. At Rs 5,999 per year, it is priced at the annual subscription level rather than per-user, making it attractive for firms wanting predictable costs.

Being extremely new (only ~2 months old as of April 2026), Finexo is still in its early stages. The 800+ firms claim is noteworthy for such a young product and suggests aggressive early marketing, though the depth of actual usage is unclear.

## Key Features
- Auto-task generation from return types (GST, ITR, TDS, etc.)

- Client portal with login
- Task management and assignment
- Compliance deadline tracking

## Integrations
- Client portal (built-in)

## Strengths
- Auto-task generation from return types -- smart workflow automation
- Website builder with client portal is a unique combo
- Annual flat pricing (not per-user)
- Early traction (800+ firms in ~2 months)
- Addresses the "setup burden" that plagues PM tool adoption

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- Extremely new (Feb 2026) -- unproven track record
- Very small team (~3-5) limits development velocity
- No Tally integration
- No WhatsApp or communication tools
- No GST/ITR auto-fetch or verification
- No billing or invoicing features
- No AI capabilities
- Feature depth is likely shallow given the age of the product
- Sustainability concerns -- can they survive on Rs 5,999/year?

## Unique Selling Points
- Auto-task generation from compliance return types
- Built-in website builder with client portal
- Very new, modern product approach

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW
- **Where they compete:** Core practice management, auto-task generation, client portal
- **Where CAOS can win:** Feature depth, integrations (Tally, WhatsApp, GST portal), maturity, team size, AI automation. Finexo is too early to be a serious threat. However, the auto-task-from-return-types feature is a good idea CAOS should consider implementing. Watch for growth trajectory.
