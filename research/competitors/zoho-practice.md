## Product Description
Zoho Practice is the practice management module from Zoho Corporation, one of India's largest SaaS companies. Launched in November 2023, it extends Zoho's extensive business suite into the accounting practice management space. The product benefits from Zoho's massive ecosystem (Zoho Books, Zoho CRM, Zoho Projects, etc.) and brand recognition.

A significant go-to-market advantage is the ICAI partnership offering 3 years free access for ICAI members. This aggressive distribution strategy leverages Zoho's deep pockets to acquire users at zero marginal cost, creating a substantial barrier for smaller competitors.

However, Zoho Practice is fundamentally a GLOBAL product adapted for India, not an India-first product. It lacks India-specific compliance features like Tally integration, GST auto-fetch, ITR verification, and WhatsApp automation. At Rs 2,950/month for 5 users, the paid pricing is premium relative to India-specific alternatives.

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** HIGH
- **Where they compete:** Brand, distribution (ICAI free offer), core practice management features, client portal, document management. Zoho's brand and free offer are powerful acquisition weapons.
- **Where CAOS can win:** India-specific compliance automation (Tally, GST, ITR, WhatsApp) -- the features Indian CAs actually need. CAOS must position as "Built for Indian CAs" vs. Zoho's "Global tool adapted for India." The lack of Tally, GST auto-fetch, and WhatsApp in Zoho Practice is CAOS's biggest competitive advantage. After the 3-year free period ends, Zoho's premium pricing (Rs 590/user vs Rs 100/user) will also drive conversions to CAOS.
