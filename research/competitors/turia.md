# Turia - Deep Dive

## Overview
- **Website:** https://turia.in
- **Category:** Practice Management
- **Founded:** ~2024
- **HQ:** Bangalore, Karnataka
- **Company:** Turia
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** 4 employees
- **Users/Customers:** 300+ CAs

## Product Description
Turia is a modern practice management system for CAs with a clean, contemporary interface. At Rs 100/user/month flat pricing, it positions itself alongside SimplifyPractice and QwikCA in the price-competitive segment of Indian CA PMS tools.

Turia differentiates with some thoughtful features like geo-tagged attendance (useful for firms where staff visit client offices), built-in SOPs and checklists (helping firms standardize their processes), and Tally integration. These features suggest the founders understand the operational challenges of running a CA firm.

With only 300+ CAs and 4 employees, Turia is still very early. The small team limits its ability to rapidly expand features and provide reliable support. However, its modern approach and Tally integration give it some unique advantages over older competitors.

## Key Features
- Task management with SOPs and checklists
- Client management

- Geo-tagged attendance (location-aware check-in/out)
- Tally integration
- Team management and workload distribution
- Document management
- Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) templates

- Mobile access

## Target Segment
- Small CA firms (3-15 people)
- CAs wanting modern, clean tools
- Firms needing Tally integration in their PMS
- Practices wanting to standardize with SOPs

## Strengths
- Modern, clean user interface
- Tally integration (rare among Indian PMS tools)
- Geo-tagged attendance for field staff tracking
- Built-in SOPs and checklists for process standardization
- Bangalore base -- tech talent access
- Thoughtful feature design

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- Extremely small team (4 employees) -- sustainability risk
- Only 300+ CAs -- very limited traction
- No WhatsApp integration
- No GST portal auto-fetch
- No payment gateway integration
- No client portal reported
- No billing or invoicing features
- No AI capabilities
- Very new and unproven
- Bootstrapped with minimal resources

## Unique Selling Points
- Geo-tagged attendance tracking
- Built-in SOPs and checklists
- Tally integration in a modern PMS
- Clean, modern UI

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW
- **Where they compete:** Modern PMS at Rs 100/user/month, Tally integration, clean UX
- **Where CAOS can win:** Feature breadth (WhatsApp, GST auto-fetch, payments, client portal), team size and resources, faster development velocity. Turia's 4-person team cannot match the feature development pace of a better-resourced competitor. However, CAOS should note the SOP/checklist and geo-tagged attendance features as good ideas to consider.
