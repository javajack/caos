- **HQ:** Ahmedabad, Gujarat
- **Company:** Practive
- **Funding:** Bootstrapped
- **Team Size:** ~10-15
- **Users/Customers:** 3,000+ users

## Product Description
Practive is an all-in-one CA office management platform that has been operating since 2015 from Ahmedabad. With 3,000+ users, it is one of the more established players in the Indian CA PMS market. The platform covers comprehensive practice management including task management, client management, compliance tracking, billing, and team management.

Practive's standout feature is its WhatsApp AI chatbot, which goes beyond simple notifications to offer an interactive AI-powered interface for CAs to interact with their practice data via WhatsApp. This is a genuinely innovative feature -- most competitors offer WhatsApp only for notifications, while Practive allows querying and management through the chatbot.

The platform also supports Razorpay and PhonePe for payment collection, custom domain client portals, and comprehensive workflow management. At Rs 75-99/user/month, it is competitively priced below the Rs 100 market standard, making it one of the most cost-effective comprehensive PMS options.

## Weaknesses & Gaps
- No Tally integration reported
- No GST portal auto-fetch reported
- No AI features beyond WhatsApp chatbot
- Bootstrapped limits growth investment
- 3K users in 9+ years is moderate growth
- No ICAI institutional partnership
- UI may be dated (operating since 2015)
- No ITR status verification

## Unique Selling Points
- WhatsApp AI chatbot (most advanced in the market)
- Razorpay + PhonePe dual payment integration
- Custom domain client portal
- Below-market pricing at Rs 75-99/user/month

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** HIGH
- **Where they compete:** Directly on practice management, WhatsApp integration, payment collection, client portal, competitive pricing. Practive is one of the most complete Indian PMS solutions and its WhatsApp AI chatbot is genuinely innovative.
- **Where CAOS can win:** Tally integration (if Practive lacks it), GST/ITR auto-fetch, modern UI redesign, deeper AI beyond just WhatsApp chatbot, compliance automation. CAOS should study Practive's WhatsApp AI chatbot closely and aim to match or exceed it. The key differentiators for CAOS would need to be Tally integration, government portal automation, and overall product experience quality. Practive is the competitor whose feature set most closely matches what CAOS likely aspires to build.
