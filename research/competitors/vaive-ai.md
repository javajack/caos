## Product Description
Vaive AI is an AI-powered GST litigation co-pilot, designed to assist tax professionals in preparing for and managing GST dispute cases. It leverages AI to analyze case facts, find relevant precedents, and help draft responses to GST notices and orders.

The company is part of the NVIDIA Inception program, which provides access to NVIDIA's AI infrastructure and resources. This suggests a serious technical foundation, though the company is extremely early-stage, having been founded just in October 2025.

Vaive AI is NOT a practice management tool. It is a highly specialized AI tool for GST litigation -- a niche within a niche. It is too early and too specialized to be a competitive concern for a practice management platform.
