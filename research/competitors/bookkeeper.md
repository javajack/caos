## Product Description
BookKeeper is a mobile-first GST billing and inventory management app with over 10 million downloads, making it one of the most downloaded business apps in India. It is designed for small businesses, shopkeepers, and traders who need a simple tool for creating GST invoices and managing inventory.

The app's key differentiator is its offline-first architecture -- it works without internet connectivity, which is critical for India's tier-2/3 cities and rural areas where connectivity is unreliable. It also offers lifetime licensing, which is unusual in the SaaS era and appeals to price-sensitive small business owners.

BookKeeper is NOT for CAs. It is a consumer/SMB billing app. With 10M+ downloads, it has massive reach but in a completely different segment from CA practice management.
