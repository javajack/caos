## Product Description
Digital Doc Sys (also known as Intelligere) is an accounting automation engine with deep Tally integration. The product converts bank statements and invoices into Tally vouchers, automating the most time-consuming part of bookkeeping for CAs and small businesses.

The company has an interesting history -- the parent entity dates back to 2002, but the current product iteration launched around 2021, suggesting a pivot or new product line. With 6,000+ MSMEs using the platform, it has meaningful traction in the small business accounting automation space.

Revenue is estimated at Rs 1-5M, indicating a small but sustainable business. The product is Tally-centric, meaning it is heavily dependent on the Tally ecosystem. It is NOT a practice management tool -- it is an accounting automation/data entry tool.

## Key Features
- Bank statement to Tally voucher conversion
- Invoice to voucher automation
- Tally ERP direct integration
- Multi-bank format support
- Data validation and error detection
- Batch processing
- Client-wise data management
- Excel/CSV import support
- Document archival
- Reconciliation assistance

## Target Segment
- MSMEs using Tally for accounting (primary)
- CA firms handling bookkeeping for Tally clients
- Accountants doing manual data entry
- Small businesses wanting to automate bookkeeping
