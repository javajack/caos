## Product Description
Karbon is the global leader in accounting practice management, with $109.4M in annual revenue and $91.8M in funding. It serves over 30,000 professionals primarily in the US, UK, Australia, and other English-speaking markets. Karbon is considered the gold standard for practice management UX and workflow design.

The platform excels in email-centric workflows, client communication management, and team collaboration. Its workflow automation, Kanban-style task management, and intelligent triage system for client requests are widely regarded as best-in-class in the global market.

However, Karbon has ZERO India-specific features. No Tally integration, no GST compliance, no ITR tracking, no WhatsApp integration. Its pricing ($59-99/user/month, or approximately Rs 5,000-8,500/user/month) is 50-85x more expensive than Indian alternatives. Karbon is relevant as a "north star" for product quality but not as a direct competitive threat in India.

## Threat Assessment for CAOS
- **Threat Level:** LOW (for India market)
- **Where they compete:** Karbon does not compete in India. Its pricing and lack of India features make it irrelevant for Indian CAs. A few large Indian firms may use it, but it is not a mass-market threat.
- **Where CAOS can win:** India-specific everything. However, CAOS should study Karbon's UX, workflow design, and product decisions extensively. Karbon is the "north star" for what a great PMS looks like. CAOS's opportunity is to build "Karbon for India" -- global-quality UX with India-specific compliance, Tally, WhatsApp, and local pricing.
